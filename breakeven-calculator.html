Understanding Break-Even Analysis

Break-Even Point is when total revenue equals total costs. You're not making profit, but you're not losing money either.

Formula: Break-Even Units = Fixed Costs √∑ (Price - Variable Cost per Unit)

  • Fixed Costs: Expenses that don't change with production (rent, salaries)
  • Variable Costs: Costs that increase with each unit sold (materials, shipping)
  • Contribution Margin: Revenue remaining after variable costs to cover fixed costs
