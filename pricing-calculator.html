Pricing Strategy Guide

Cost-Plus (Markup): Add percentage to cost. Simple, guarantees profit.

Profit Margin: Set desired profit as % of price. More strategic for competitive markets.

Competitive Pricing: Price based on competitors while maintaining profitability.

Common Markup by Industry:

  • Groceries: 10-20%
  • Clothing: 100-150%
  • Electronics: 20-40%
  • Handmade/Custom: 200-400%
  • Digital Products: 500-1000%+
