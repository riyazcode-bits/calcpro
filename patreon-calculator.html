How Patreon Works

Patreon is a membership platform that allows creators to earn recurring income from their supporters (patrons).

  • Membership Tiers: Offer different reward levels at various price points
  • Recurring Revenue: Patrons pay monthly for ongoing access to exclusive content
  • Platform Fees: Patreon takes 5-12% depending on your plan, plus payment processing (~2.9% + $0.30)
  • Lite Plan (5%): Basic tools, community, business insights
  • Pro Plan (8%): + Unlimited app integrations, analytics, promotion
  • Premium Plan (12%): + Priority support, team accounts, merch for membership

Note: This calculator accounts for Patreon's platform fee and estimated payment processing fees. Actual fees may vary slightly based on patron location and payment method.

Tips to Grow Your Patreon

  • Clear Value Proposition: Explain exactly what patrons get at each tier
  • Start Small: Begin with 2-3 tiers, add more as you grow
  • Price Strategically: Common tiers: $3-5 (entry), $10-15 (mid), $25+ (premium)
  • Deliver Consistently: Set realistic goals and always meet them
  • Exclusive Content: Behind-the-scenes, early access, patron-only posts
  • Engage Your Community: Polls, Q&As, patron shoutouts
  • Promote Regularly: Mention Patreon in every video/post/stream
  • Show Impact: Share how patron support helps your work
  • Offer Flexibility: Annual billing discounts, pause options
