Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

At CalcPro, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our calculator tools.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect personal information. CalcPro is designed to respect your privacy:

  • All calculations happen in your browser (client-side)
  • We do not store your calculation data
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers

Analytics & Cookies

We use basic analytics to improve our service:

  • Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about page views and calculator usage
  • No Tracking: We do not track individual users across sessions
  • Cookies: We may use minimal cookies for basic functionality only

Third-Party Services

CalcPro may use third-party services for:

  • Hosting: Vercel (privacy policy: vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Analytics: Basic anonymous metrics only
  • Advertising: Google AdSense (if applicable)

Data Security

Since all calculations happen in your browser and we don't store your data:

  • Your calculation inputs are never sent to our servers
  • Your financial data remains completely private
  • No database stores your information

Children's Privacy

CalcPro does not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13. Our services are intended for general audiences.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@calcpro.com
